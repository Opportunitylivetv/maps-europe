406, Advance of the Huns

406 AD

McEvedy shows the northern part of Pannonia, around Lake Balaton, as Hunnic territory in 406 AD, whereas all of several other maps I looked at show the Roman border intact. Since Attila the Hun was apparently born in Pannonia around 406, I'm led to think that McEvedy was right, but to hedge my bets I overlapped the two territories. The book i'm reading about Attila the Hun indicates that the Huns were allowed to slowly settle that area over the period in question by Aetius.
