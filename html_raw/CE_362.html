362, Ostrogoth expansion, arrival of the Huns

336 The Ostrogoths absorb the Bosporan Kingdom.

c350 The Huns push from the east as far as the Volga.

by 362 The Ostrogoths reputedly rule an empire under King Ermanarich (Herman) that covers not only the Pontic steppe, but reaches back to their homeland on the Baltic.

The Slav territories expand to the cost of the Balts, and the Finns move into their present-day homeland.

The New Penguin Atlas of Medieval History, Colin McEvedy, pp 10-11
