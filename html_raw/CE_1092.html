1092, Seljuk Anatolia, Almoravids in Spain

1072-5 Alfonso IV of Leon acquires Castille and Galicia.

1077 Pisa adds Corsica to Sardinian protectorship on encouragement by the Pope.

1085 Toledo captured by Christian forces.

1087 William the Conqueror dies, and the Duchy of Normandy reverts to France.

1091 Roger d'Hauteville completes the conquest of Sicily. Hungary acquires Croatia.

1092 With the annexation of Cumbria, the border with Scotland assume its final form.

1090-2 The Almovarids, summoned from North African to help stop the Christian advance, acquire almost all the Spanish emirates.
