1130, The Kingdom of Jerusalem, and other consolidations

1106 Unable to redeem the Duchy of Normandy after returning from the crusades, the Duke cedes it to the King of England.

1110-5 Almoravids complete their conquest of Spanish emirates.

1112 Barcelona and Provence are united.

1118 Almoravids lose Zaragoza to Navarre.

1121 The Georgians conquer Tblisi as the Seljuk power wanes, and go on to liberate other lands inhabited by Georgian peoples.

1124 After 22 years the Polish state subdues the Pomeranians.

1126 Novgorod becomes a 'republic'.

1127 Roger of Sicily acquires the Duchy of Apulia.

by 1130 The Seljuk empire is disintegrating into many small fiefdoms. Crusader lands are consolidated into a Kingdom of Jerusalem, with subordinate principality and counties in Antioch, Edessa and Tripoli.

The Cumans absorb the remnants of the Patzinaks.

The New Penguin Atlas of Medieval History, Colin McEvedy, pp 66-67
